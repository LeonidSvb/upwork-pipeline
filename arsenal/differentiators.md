### Email templates that work

**Template: Signal-based (short, direct)**
Use when: ICP is specific, there's a clear trigger (hiring, expansion, funding, new location)

```
Hey [First name],

your name came up on my end,

got [X relevant buyers / leads / partners] looking for [their service/product] — thought you might be a fit

worth a quick chat?
```

Example (drone detection):
```
Hey Ian,

your name came up on my end,

got a few airport authorities looking for a portable drone detection system — thought you might be a fit

worth a quick chat?
```

---

**Template: Peer-tone volume play**
Use when: ICP is a founder/head at SMB or agency, want casual + data signal combo
Tone rule: casual peer, not sales pitch. Capitalize I and first word of sentence only. Simple hyphen not em dash.

```
I spend a lot of time talking to [job_titles] at [company_type] — so I usually know who's actively looking before it hits the market.

Most [niche descriptor] hit [season/event] with [common pain point].

[1 line soft CTA]
```

**Filled: Cold Email Agency / Outbound SaaS context**
Variables: job_titles = heads of growth, VP Sales, founders | company_type = B2B SaaS startups, Series A companies | niche = cold email agencies, outbound teams

```
I spend a lot of time talking to heads of growth at Series A SaaS companies — so I usually know who's actively looking for outbound help before they post a job.

Most cold email agencies hit Q2 with a full client roster but a thin new business pipeline of their own.

Worth a quick chat to see if there's a fit?
```
