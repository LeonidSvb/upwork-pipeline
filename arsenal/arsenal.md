## Cover Letter Formula

**Structure:**
1. Puncher line (Unicode bold 𝗕𝗼𝗹𝗱) — "I've done exactly this: [specific result]"
2. 2-3 relevant case study references (числа, ниша, срок)
3. Loom video mention — "I recorded a 3-min Loom showing [specific thing]"
4. 1-2 smart questions that show you understood the job deeply
5. Speed advantage — "I already have [workflow/template/system] built, so this takes days not weeks"
6. Soft CTA — "Happy to jump on a quick call or send more details"
7. If job says "start with X words" — always do it

**Puncher line formulas:**
- "𝗜'𝘃𝗲 𝗯𝘂𝗶𝗹𝘁 𝗲𝘅𝗮𝗰𝘁𝗹𝘆 𝘁𝗵𝗶𝘀 — [result] for [niche] in [timeframe]"
